# Portfolio Risk Checklist

## Concentration

- Single-name exposure.
- Sector and industry exposure.
- Country, currency, and rate sensitivity.
- Factor crowding: growth, value, quality, momentum, beta, leverage.
- Liquidity and market-cap concentration.

## Common Drivers

Look beyond ticker count. Multiple positions may share the same driver:

- Rates and duration.
- Commodity prices.
- FX.
- China, US, Europe, or emerging-market demand.
- AI capex, consumer cycle, housing cycle, credit cycle.

## Scenarios

- Earnings miss.
- Multiple compression.
- Liquidity shock.
- Regulatory or policy event.
- Recession.
- Sector rotation.

## Controls

- Define review triggers before loss events.
- Align sizing with thesis confidence and volatility.
- Treat hedge ideas as research inputs unless implementation details are specified.
