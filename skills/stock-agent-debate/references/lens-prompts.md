# Stock Debate Lens Prompts

Use only lenses that match the user's question.

## Fundamental

- What is the business quality?
- Are margins, returns, and cash flow durable?
- Is the balance sheet strong enough for the downside case?

## Valuation

- What expectations are implied by the current price?
- Which valuation metrics are appropriate for this sector?
- Where can the valuation be misleading?

## Technical

- What is the trend and momentum context?
- Are volume and volatility confirming or contradicting the move?
- Is the technical signal relevant to the user's horizon?

## Macro

- Which rates, FX, commodity, policy, or cycle variables matter?
- Are macro risks already reflected in the price?

## Sentiment

- What changed in news, transcripts, estimates, or positioning?
- Are sentiment signals sourced and dated?

## Risk

- What breaks the thesis?
- What single data error would reverse the conclusion?
- What position or portfolio risk does this create?
